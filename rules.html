Prizes Scoring Rules About Home

  • Participants are required to participate in a group of three members.
  • Participants cannot discuss or share code with other team members.
  • If similar code submission is found between two teams, both teams are disqualified.
  • It is a six-hour long hackathon,twelve problems are to be solved in the given time frame.
  • The hackathon is divided into four quarters (Q1, Q2, Q3, Q4) and each quarter is one and a half hours long.
  • The contest starts with only three visible problems in the first quarter, that is all other(nine) problems are hidden.
  • As we enter each quarter additional three problems are made visible to the user.
  • We have frame the problems so that the difficulty is balanced for all the participants with different expertise
  • The hackathon will be hosted on HackerRank platform.
